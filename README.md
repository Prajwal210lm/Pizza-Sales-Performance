

# Pizza Sales Performance Analysis

**SQL + Excel Dashboard Project**

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **pizza sales performance** using **SQL for data querying and transformation** and **Excel for dashboarding and visualization**. The objective is to derive **actionable business insights** around revenue, demand patterns, customer preferences, and product performance.

The **Excel dashboard is a direct reflection of the SQL analysis**, ensuring consistency across tools. The underlying data used in Excel and SQL is the same.

---

## Business Problem Statement

The business wants to understand its pizza sales performance in order to:

* Track key revenue and order metrics
* Identify peak sales periods (days and hours)
* Understand customer preferences by pizza category and size
* Identify best-selling and underperforming pizzas
* Support data-driven decisions for operations, pricing, and promotions

---

## KPIs Analyzed

The following key performance indicators were calculated:

1. **Total Revenue**
   Sum of total price of all pizza orders

2. **Average Order Value (AOV)**
   Total revenue ÷ total number of orders

3. **Total Pizzas Sold**
   Sum of quantities of all pizzas sold

4. **Total Orders**
   Count of unique orders placed

5. **Average Pizzas per Order**
   Total pizzas sold ÷ total orders

## Charts & Analysis Performed

### 1. Daily Trend of Total Orders

* Bar chart showing order volume by day of the week
* Helps identify high-demand days
* Insight: Orders peak on **weekends**, especially **Friday and Saturday**

### 2. Hourly Trend of Total Orders

* Line chart showing order distribution across hours of the day
* Identifies peak ordering hours
* Insight: Highest orders occur between **12–1 PM** and **4–8 PM**

### 3. Percentage of Sales by Pizza Category

* Pie chart representing sales contribution by category
* Categories analyzed: Classic, Chicken, Supreme, Veggie
* Insight: **Classic pizzas** contribute the highest share of total sales

### 4. Percentage of Sales by Pizza Size

* Pie chart showing customer preference by size
* Sizes analyzed: Small, Medium, Large, X-Large, XX-Large
* Insight: **Large size pizzas** dominate sales, indicating higher average ticket sizes

### 5. Total Pizzas Sold by Category

* Funnel / bar chart comparing total pizzas sold per category
* Insight: Classic category leads, followed by Supreme and Veggie

### 6. Top 5 Best-Selling Pizzas

* Bar chart highlighting pizzas with highest quantities sold
* Insight: Certain classic and chicken-based pizzas are strong revenue drivers

### 7. Bottom 5 Worst-Selling Pizzas

* Bar chart identifying underperforming items
* Insight: Some niche pizzas consistently underperform and may need repositioning or removal

---

## Dashboard Summary Insights

* **Revenue Performance:** Strong revenue driven by high order volumes and preference for large pizzas
* **Customer Behavior:** Customers prefer classic flavors and larger sizes
* **Operational Timing:** Clear lunch and evening peaks can guide staffing and inventory planning
* **Product Strategy:** Best sellers can be promoted further, while worst sellers can be optimized or discontinued
